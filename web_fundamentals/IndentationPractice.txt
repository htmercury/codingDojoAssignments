<html> is root and has no parent
<html> children include <head> and <body>

<head> has one child <title>
<title> has content "Basic II"

<body> children include <table>, <h1>, and <ul>

<table> children include <thead>, <tbody>
<thead> has sibling <tbody>
<thead> has child <tr>
<tbody> has child <tr>

<h1> has content "Here is a list of my favorite things:"
<ul> has children <li>
